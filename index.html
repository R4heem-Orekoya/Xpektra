About Me

Hello, I'm Ogungbayi Imran Majekodunmi, but you can call me Xpektra - your go-to web developer and designer. With over 2 years of freelancing experience, I've developed a strong foundation in crafting exceptional digital experiences. As an Electrical Engineering student at Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and creative flair to my work. I'm passionate about creating visually stunning and user-friendly websites that drive results. With a methodical approach and a love for problem-solving, I deliver top-notch solutions that meet my clients' needs. I'm always seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow, both as a professional and as an individual. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
